Quick and Easy Rice
I am by no means a master chef, the number of things I'm able to cook can probably be counted on 1 hand, I've even managed to burn water before, but here is a quick and easy way to make a perfect bowl of rice. What you will need:
Ingredients
  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 3 Cups of Water
  • 2 Tblsp of Butter
  • 2 Pinches of Kosher Salt
Cookware
  • 2-3 qt Saucepan for Rice
  • Kettle or Pot for Water
  • Spatula
  • A Timer
  • Sheet of foil (Optional)
